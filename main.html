Jammu - The City of Temples


Jammu, often referred to as the "City of Temples," is a beautiful and culturally rich destination in northern India. It is known for its stunning landscapes, historical significance, and spiritual aura. Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, Jammu offers breathtaking views, serene environments, and a unique blend of history and spirituality.

Top 5 Attractions in Jammu

  1. Vaishno Devi Temple - One of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in India.
  2. Raghunath Temple - A magnificent temple dedicated to Lord Rama.
  3. Bahu Fort - A historic fort with the beautiful Bagh-e-Bahu garden.
  4. Mansar Lake - A picturesque lake surrounded by lush greenery.
  5. Shiv Khori - A mystical cave shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Must-Try Foods in Jammu

  • Rajma Chawal - A delicious dish made with red kidney beans and rice.
  • Kaladi Kulcha - A traditional cheese dish with a crispy texture.
  • Aloo Anardana - A tangy potato dish with pomegranate seeds.
  • Khatta Meat - A unique mutton dish with a sour and spicy flavor.
  • Dogri Maa Ki Dal - A flavorful lentil dish made with black gram.
